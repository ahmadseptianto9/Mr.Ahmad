Attack on Titan

Original title: Shingeki no kyojin

Creator: Yasuko Kobayashi and Hiroshi Seko

Stars: Jessie James Grelle, Bryce Paperbrook, Trina Nishimura

Synopsis

Attack on Titan follows Eren Jaeger, who vows to exterminate man-eating Titans after they destroy his town and kill his mother. Set in a world where humanity is confined behind giant walls, Eren, alongside friends Mikasa and Armin, joins the military to fight Titans, unraveling dark conspiracies and fighting for freedom.
